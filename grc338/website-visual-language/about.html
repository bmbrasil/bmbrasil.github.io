Higuera House of Flowers began with a simple love for color, texture, and the quiet beauty found in nature. I have always believed that flowers carry a unique ability to lift spirits and create meaningful moments. This studio was created to share that feeling with the community and to offer arrangements that feel thoughtful, personal, and full of life.

My work is inspired by the charm and warmth of San Luis Obispo. I love exploring seasonal blooms, mixing soft and bold textures, and designing pieces that reflect the calm and creativity of the Central Coast. Every arrangement is crafted with attention and intention, whether it is for a celebration, a gesture of comfort, or a moment of joy.

Being located on Higuera Street is something that brings me so much happiness. The daily energy of downtown and the friendly faces that stop by always inspire my designs. I find influence in the colors of our sunsets, the peaceful spirit of our community, and the natural beauty that surrounds this place I call home.

My hope is that every bouquet brings a little light into your day. Flowers can express what words sometimes cannot, and I feel grateful each time I get to create something meaningful for someone. Thank you for supporting small and local. I am truly excited to share more beautiful blooms with you.
